About Us

Founded by Ronald, our company is driven by the vision of empowering individuals and businesses to achieve financial success. Ronald’s journey began in the heart of Harare, Zimbabwe, where he grew up with a passion for technology and finance. After years of working in the industry and gaining a deep understanding of financial markets, Ronald launched the Loan Origination System, which has since revolutionized loan processes globally.

Today, we are proud to operate out of major cities around the world, including:

  • New York, USA
  • London, UK
  • Sydney, Australia
  • Hong Kong
  • Harare, Zimbabwe
  • São Paulo, Brazil

With Ronald's leadership and the collective expertise of our international team, we continue to push boundaries in financial technology, ensuring seamless experiences for our users worldwide.
